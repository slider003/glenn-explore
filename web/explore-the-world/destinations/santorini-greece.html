Frequently Asked Questions

What can I see in the virtual Santorini tour?

Our virtual tour offers immersive experiences of Santorini's most iconic locations, including the white-washed villages of Oia and Fira, stunning caldera views, and famous sunset spots. The AI guide provides historical context, architectural insights, and local cultural information throughout your journey.

How accurate is the virtual Santorini experience?

Our virtual recreation of Santorini is highly detailed and accurate, thanks to advanced WebGL and ThreeJS technology combined with Mapbox's precise terrain data. While it's a virtual experience, it provides an authentic sense of the island's unique architecture, dramatic landscapes, and Mediterranean atmosphere.

Can I use this to plan my actual visit to Santorini?

Absolutely! Many travelers use our virtual tour to plan their Santorini trips. The AI guide provides practical information about best visiting times, photography spots, local customs, and must-see locations. You can explore different villages, discover hidden gems, and learn about the island's unique features before your physical visit.
