Frequently Asked Questions

What can I see in the virtual Bali tour?

Our virtual tour offers immersive experiences of Bali's most iconic locations, including ancient temples, rice terraces, pristine beaches, and traditional villages. The AI guide provides cultural insights, historical context, and local customs information throughout your journey.

How accurate is the virtual Bali experience?

Our virtual recreation of Bali is highly detailed and accurate, thanks to advanced WebGL and ThreeJS technology combined with Mapbox's precise terrain data. While it's a virtual experience, it provides an authentic sense of the island's landscapes, architecture, and cultural elements.

Can I use this to plan my actual visit to Bali?

Absolutely! Many travelers use our virtual tour to plan their Bali trips. The AI guide provides practical information about best visiting times, cultural etiquette, local customs, and must-see locations. You can explore different regions of the island and learn about local traditions before your physical visit.
